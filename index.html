ILYAVIEW COMMUNITY WEB SITE

The Ilyaview Township was established in 1903 to preserve the history and heritage of our area and community founders. With our elevated prairie location and unobstructed horizons, Ilyaview has served generations of visitors as the perfect place to sit back, watch the endless skies, and wait for Saskatchewan's legendary sunsets and seasonal spectacles.

Our community is a tapestry of stories, woven together by the experiences of those who have called this place home. The St. Ilya Orthodox Church, built in 1917, remains the spiritual heart of our community
